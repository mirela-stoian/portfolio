Workshop at
Refresh Art Gallery Project
my role: concept, design, coordination

The workshop took place in 2014 at Kulturfabrikken in Nykøbing Falster, Denmark.

The idea behind the workshop was to encourage a young audience to ponder upon the written communication that surrounds them in their everyday life, and to express themselves through the means of arts and crafts.

The workshop was hold in English and had a duration of three hours. The aim was to encourage the teens that participated to develop their language skills and their critical thinking in an engaging, hands-on manner and in the company of others. The results were displayed on site, as a temporary exhibition.
