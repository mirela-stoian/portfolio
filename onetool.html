One Tool

Informative material
my role: illustration, layout design

One Tool is a project I worked on in 2019 and my role was to visually design a poster and a three fold leaflet. The materials were aimed to be used in hospitals. The target audience were medical professionals and their patients. The aim of the project was to provide information regarding coping strategies while living a life with a stoma condition.

color palette

The main insight that stayed at the base for creating these materials was that being able to find a word, more precisly - a name, for the stoma, made it more accessible to communicate both physical changes or events, and feelings correlated with living with this medical condition.
