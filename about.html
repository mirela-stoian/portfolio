About and resume

As a designer

I am passionate about creating products that are intuitive, seamless and enjoyable to use. I strive to become better at what I do and I am enthusiatic to learn new things.
‍
Active listening, taking notes and sketching are at the essence of my conceptualising process. I am a natural problem solver and I complement that through practice. My ability to convert complex information into easy to understand design, to communicate the logic of my design decisions and my passion for accessibility, were most appreciated by colleagues from previous workplaces.

My professional experience comes from working in cross-functional teams and during my M.Sc. education in media technologies at Aalborg University Copenhagen I had the opportunity to experiment with IxD, Computational Design and AI.
