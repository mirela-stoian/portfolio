Kune Kbh

Experimental graphics for music events
my role: concept, illustration, layout design

I have collaborated with the collective Kune Kbh (Kune Kbh Facebook page) in 2017 and created graphics for more of their music events that took place in various locations.


The graphic style varies between minimalist and eclectic, and the execution is done based on a sensorial approach: listening the music the event was promoting,  visiting the location in advance or having creative brainstorming sessions with the organisers.
